3 Most Popular Online Games in the Philippines

Defense of the Ancients (DotA)

Dota 2 is a multiplayer online battle arena (MOBA) video game developed and published by Valve. He game is a sequel to Defense of the Ancients (DotA), which was a community-created mod for Blizzard Entertainment's Warcraft III: Reign of Chaos and its expansion pack, The Frozen Throne. Dota 2 is played in matches between two teams of five players, with each team occupying and defending their own separate base on the map.

League of Legends (LoL)

In League of Legends, players form a team of five and assume the role of a "champion", characters with unique abilities, generally varying around a type of class, and battle against a team of player or computer controlled champions. In the main game mode, Summoner's Rift, the goal is to destroy the opposing team's "Nexus".

Mobile Legends: Bang Bang (ML)

Mobile Legends: Bang Bang is a multiplayer online battle arena (MOBA) game designed for mobile phones. The two opposing teams fight to reach and destroy the enemy's base while defending their own base for control of a path, the three "lanes" known as "top", "middle", and "bottom", which connects the bases.
